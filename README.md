## тЬи What makes it special

### ЁЯОз Hands-Free (HF) тАФ learn languages while you talk
Hold **Alt+T** to speak. Release to send. The AI replies in your target language with its face reacting to every word тАФ embarrassed, curious, delighted. No typing. No clicking. Just conversation.

> *"I use it to practice Japanese. The pumpkin face going SURPRISED every time I say something wrong is weirdly motivating."*

### ЁЯза 4 MB emotion brain тАФ runs at 60 ms on your GPU
TinyBERT INT8 ONNX, trained on 3 200 sentences across 8 emotions. Runs via WebGPU in Chrome тАФ no Python, no server, no API key for inference. The face reacts to the AI's output, not yours.

### ЁЯОи 40+ pixel-art characters тАФ or submit your own
![Character gallery тАФ Dev, Daruma, Console, Terminator](docs/screenshots/3_characters.png)

Drop a sprite sheet in `sprites/art/yourname/` and run one sync script. Your character appears instantly. [Submit it to us](#submit-your-art) for priority review and a place in the official gallery.

### ЁЯСБя╕П Eye tracking тАФ opt-in, on-device
MediaPipe face landmarker runs locally. The pupils follow your gaze. No video ever leaves your machine.

### ЁЯФК Voice I/O тАФ bot mode, natural mode, or silent
- **Bot mode**: typewriter sound while the AI streams
- **Natural mode**: browser TTS reads the reply aloud
- **HF mode**: hold Alt+T тЖТ speak тЖТ auto-send тЖТ AI replies

### ЁЯФТ Privacy-proof
Zero network calls for inference. Your API key lives in `localStorage`, never transmitted to us. GPL-3.0 тАФ read every line.
